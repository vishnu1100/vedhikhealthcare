Our Services

At our medical shop, we are committed to providing high-quality products and services that meet your healthcare needs. We adhere to strict quality standards and regulations to ensure that our products are safe and effective.

Pharmacy

Our pharmacy facility is dedicated to providing high-quality, convenient, and affordable pharmaceutical services to our customers. We offer a wide range of prescription medications, over-the-counter medications, and other healthcare products to meet the needs of our customers.Also home delivery available.

Blood Tests

Blood test home delivery facility is committed to providing our patients with a convenient, reliable, and safe way to get their blood tests done. We strive to ensure that our patients receive the highest quality care and support and are able to manage their health and wellness needs with ease and confidence, ensuring that all test results are kept confidential and are only shared with authorized individuals.

Counsultant Doctor

Our consultant doctors work closely with patients to evaluate their symptoms and medical history and develop individualized treatment plans that address their specific needs and concerns. They use the latest diagnostic tools and techniques to identify underlying health conditions and recommend appropriate treatment options.

Equipement Rental

Rental equipment is carefully maintained and serviced to ensure that it is always in top condition and ready for use. We offer flexible rental periods to meet the specific needs of our customers, whether they need equipment for a short-term project or a long-term rental.

Palliative Care

Our palliative care facility is committed to providing our patients with compassionate and comprehensive care that supports their physical, emotional, and spiritual needs. We strive to help our patients and their families navigate this difficult time with as much comfort, dignity, and support as possible.

Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy facility provides specialized care and support for individuals who are experiencing pain, injury, or disability related to their muscles, bones, or joints. Our team of experienced physiotherapists work closely with patients to develop personalized treatment plans that address their specific needs and concerns.
